What I Do

Composition

I specialize in (but of course am not limited to) JRPG-style music, which is to say, highly melodic and narrative-driven music in the style of Final Fantasy, the Chrono series, Tales series and so on. What made these games special wasn’t just that their music was good; their music had an identity.

Likewise, my standard is to make music that is a selling point and integral part of the identity of the games I work on, not just inoffensive but forgettable background noise. I strive to make music that will help your game become a cherished and significant emotional experience for its players.

Arrangement

Do you have a song you've written that you'd like arranged for as a solo piano, orchestral or even symphonic rock piece, but lack either the time or skills to do so?

I can help with that.

About

My name’s Geoff Butterworth, also known as Vermanubis, and it’s a pleasure to meet you.

Game music has been my life since I was 5 years old when I would play all the way through Final Fantasy: Mystic Quest just to jam to the final dungeon theme. As I got older, I started to realize that game music got into my heart and made me feel in ways no other kind of music came close to.

Since about that age it's been my life's work to understand and perfect what makes game music so special and to get others to feel what my most beloved games made me feel in my own work. If you can see that fact reflected in my work and want music that will stay with your players forever, I can't overstate how happy I'd be to oblige and work with you to make something that'll mean something to people.
